<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>Atour Lifestyle Holdings Limited Releases
2025 Environmental, Social and Governance Report</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">SHANGHAI, April 30, 2026 /GlobeNewswire/ -- Atour
Lifestyle Holdings Limited (&ldquo;Atour&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) (NASDAQ: ATAT), a leading lifestyle group in China, today
announced the release of its 2025 Environmental, Social and Governance (&ldquo;ESG&rdquo;) report. The report outlines Atour&rsquo;s
management philosophy and key achievements across environmental stewardship, social responsibility, and governance practices for the
year ended December 31, 2025, reflecting the Company&rsquo;s deepening integration of sustainability into its long-term development.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Throughout 2025, the Company advanced its ESG
governance framework and made meaningful progress in key areas, including customer experience, employee development, franchisee and supply
chain collaboration, green operations, and community engagement. Embarking on this new chapter, Atour will continue to steadily advance
sustainable development, working toward its long-term vision: &ldquo;A Timeless Atour, Warmth Along Every Journey.&rdquo; The Company
will fulfill its firm and warm promise to customers and continue to contribute its solid and enduring strengths to all stakeholders.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">To learn more and download the full ESG report,
please visit: <U>https://ir.yaduo.com/about-us/esg</U>.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Atour Lifestyle Holdings Limited</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Atour Lifestyle Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: ATAT)
is a leading lifestyle group in China that operates both hospitality and retail businesses. As a leader in quality living, Atour is dedicated
to creating an intimate ambiance where people can warmly connect. Guided by its people-serving philosophy, Atour continuously refines
its products and services to curate exceptional experiences for every user.</P>
